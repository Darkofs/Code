About Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional football (soccer) player widely regarded as one of the greatest of all time. Born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, Messi began playing football at a young age and joined the youth team of his local club, Newell's Old Boys, at the age of eight. However, due to a medical condition (growth hormone deficiency), he moved to Spain to join FC Barcelona's renowned youth academy, La Masia, at the age of 13.
